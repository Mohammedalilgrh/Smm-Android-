2. How We Use Your Information

  • Execute scheduled posts on your behalf
  • Maintain and improve our service
  • Communicate service updates and support
3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • OAuth 2.0 for social media connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
5. Social Media Integration

When you connect social accounts, we receive limited permissions to post content on your behalf. We only access information necessary for posting and do not read private messages or personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time, which will remove all associated data within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We use essential cookies for authentication and service functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Disconnect social media accounts

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect service changes or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through our support channels.
